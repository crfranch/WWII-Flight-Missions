World War II Flight Missions

This is a dataset consisting of flight missions during World War II.


This dataset contains 100,000 + missions. The features/information (columns) about the missions include:



    WWII_ID, MASTER_INDEX_NUMBER, MSNDATE, THEATER, NAF, COUNTRY_FLYING_MISSION, TGT_COUNTRY_CODE, TGT_COUNTRY, TGT_LOCATION, TGT_TYPE, TGT_ID, TGT_INDUSTRY_CODE, TGT_INDUSTRY, SOURCE_LATITUDE, SOURCE_LONGITUDE, LATITUDE, LONGITUDE, UNIT_ID, MDS, AIRCRAFT_NAME, MSN_TYPE, TGT_PRIORITY, TGT_PRIORITY_EXPLANATION, AC_ATTACKING, ALTITUDE, ALTITUDE_FEET, NUMBER_OF_HE, NUMBER_OF_IC, TYPE_OF_IC, LBS_IC, TONS_OF_IC, NUMBER_OF_FRAG, TYPE_OF_FRAG, LBS_FRAG, TONS_OF_FRAG, TOTAL_TONS, TAKEOFF_BASE, TAKEOFF_COUNTRY, TAKEOFF_LATITUDE FLOAT, TAKEOFF_LONGITUDE, BDA, CALLSIGN, ROUNDS_AMMO FLOAT and YEAR.
